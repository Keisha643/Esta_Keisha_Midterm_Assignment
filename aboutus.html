About Us

Swerve began as a small-batch cocktail brand built on experimentation and flavour innovation. Over decades, the brand became synonymous with nightlife, social connection, and bold taste. In 2016, Swerve “swerved” into a new lane — sports hydration — bringing its flavour expertise to athletes seeking performance without compromise. This dual identity defines Swerve today: vibrant, adaptable, and always in motion.

At Swerve, we believe great drinks should feel effortless. That’s why our cocktails are crisp, refreshing, and made to fit any moment — from spontaneous nights out to slow afternoons with friends. We’re here to bring flavour, energy, and a little attitude to every sip.
